Find Your Fit

Is Revive Right for You?

Revive Veneers work for most people, but not everyone. They're made for healthy teeth you want to look better, not for treating dental problems. Here's an honest look so you can decide.

Designed for you if:
  • You have generally healthy teeth and gums
  • You want to improve the look of stained, slightly gapped, or worn teeth
  • You have all of your upper or all of your lower teeth
Works for you, with a longer design timeline:
  • Small to medium gaps between teeth
  • Minor bite differences (slight overbite or underbite)
  • Worn or short teeth from grinding
  • One or two missing teeth, not at the end of the arch
Check with your own dentist first if:
  • You have active gum disease or significant gum recession
  • You have a severe bite misalignment
  • You've had major dental work in the last 3 months
  • You're missing several teeth, or teeth at the end of the arch
Revive isn't the right fit if:
  • You have no remaining teeth in the arch you want covered
  • You currently wear full dentures
  • You have an active oral infection or untreated gum disease

Not sure? Take our free quiz. We'll tell you honestly.

Revive Veneers are cosmetic appliances, not medical devices. They enhance the appearance of your smile but do not treat dental conditions. Dr. Brandon Turley, DDS, recommends consulting a licensed dentist for any underlying dental health concerns before ordering.
